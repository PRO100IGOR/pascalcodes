Feed Reader Wizard

You can use this wizard to subscribe to RSS or ATOM feeds, and display them in Editor status bar.

Feeds Display

The Titles from feeds will be shown dynamically as hyper link in Editor status bar. Click to open link in browser.

Three buttons will appear when mouse move in, which means Previous Item/Settings/Next Item.

Right click to popup context menu.

  • Previous Item: Show Previous feed content item.

  • Next Item: Show Next feed content item.

  • Update All: Update all subscribed feeds.

  • Close: Close this Wizard.

  • Settings: Open settings dialog.

 

Feed Reader Settings

Feed Reader Settings Dialog is shown as below:

  • Subscribe CnPack channels: Whether to subscribe to CnPack channels. Recommend to subscribe.

  • Subscribe CnPack Partner channels: Whether to subscribe to CnPack Partner channels. Recommend to subscribe.

  • Random display: Display items randomly or in sequence.

  • Change Period: The time interval to switch items.

  • Feed List: Customized feed list. Both RSS and ATOM supported.

    • Add: Add a new feed.

    • Delete: Delete selected feed.

    • Caption: The caption of selected feed. Only use to display here.

    • Url: Feed url. Both RSS and ATOM supported.

    • Period: The time interval to update feed.

    • Limit: Limited count of display. 0 means unlimited.

  • OK: Save settings and close dialog.

  • Cancel: Cancel settings and close dialog.

  • Help: Show this Help.
